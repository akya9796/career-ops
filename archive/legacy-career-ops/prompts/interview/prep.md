# Interview Preparation Prompt

Generate role-specific interview preparation from sourced facts only.

Include:

- Likely interview questions.
- STAR stories grounded in CV facts.
- Product, business analysis, API, technical, and system design questions where relevant.
- Company research placeholders when research has not been performed.
- 30/60/90 day plan when useful.
- Facts used, confidence, and hallucination risk.
